My primary music interest lies in a genre known as psychedelic trance (psytrance). In particular, my favorite artist is Astrix, though I also enjoy Ace Venture, Ritmo, Infected Mushroom, Pixel, Alpha Portal, and yet others. Infected Mushroom used to be my favorite music group, but once, when reading about them, I found out that they originally produced a genre called "psytrance". I looked the word up and saw that Astrix was a prominent influence in the genre as well. One late night, I played a couple of his songs on a speaker out of curiosity, and instantly, I loved what I heard. I was enchanted by the mysterious atmosphere of Deep Jungle Walk , the colorful melodies of He.Art, in awe of the musical epic that is Shamanic Tales, and moved by the vivid and driving beat of Valley of Stevie.
